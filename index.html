What We Are

Not a Startup. An Origination Engine.

The word 'startup' implies a single product, a single market, and a single bet. AariNAT is none of these.

01 · Identify
We find broken sectors
Nigeria's most persistent economic failures share a structure: large, predictable, failing for decades, with no serious private-sector solution. That combination is the definition of an investable opportunity.
02 · Design
We build the framework
Capital structure. SPV model. Regulatory map. Risk register. Governance model. Origination economics. Each framework is stress-tested by independent financial reviewers before investor engagement begins.
03 · Originate
We raise the capital
We introduce frameworks to DFIs, pension funds, and strategic investors. We execute Origination Recognition Agreements. We earn our origination fee at close — before a single transaction occurs in the operating company.
04 · Hold & Earn
We earn at every stage
Board seat in the operating company. Management fee from Phase 1. Equity stake that appreciates toward exit. Each new initiative adds a new income stream without reducing the value of existing ones.
